"Promoting Good Electrical Power For A Better Nigeria"

The Nigerian International Energy Summit 2022

we urge you to make plans to attend the upcoming Nigerian International Energy Summit, an in-person event set to reunite our national communities, continue to power our Energy industry's recovery, and create a better electrical situation in the country.

2022.04.13(WED) ~ 16(SAT)

@ The State House | ICC, Abuja, Nigeria

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the minister of power and other notable figures in the the power sector give update on the current electrical situation of the country.

Forum

Ask question, have discussions with the country's leading power officials here to guide you and answer all questions you might have.

Workshop

Network with top tech leaders accross the country. Display the country's future electrical system.

Exhibition

Exhibition of the transformations taken place in the country's power sector.

Awards

Rewarding and recognising the top players in the country's energy sector.
