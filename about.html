The Nigerian International Energy Summit 2022

In the spirit of the global agenda for energy transition and the need for us to remain reelevant and ahead, The Nigerian International Petroleum Summit quietly did a rebrand. From this moment, it'll be known as The Nigerian International Energy Summit.
With the ongoing reforms in the sector and improved engagement with host communities, Nigeria is positioning herself to be the foremost energy investment destination. The official Nigerian International Energy Summit continues to create the perfect platform for the world to meet Nigeria's energy industry players.
